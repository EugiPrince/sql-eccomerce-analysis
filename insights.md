# Business Insights

## Customer Behavior
- A small percentage of customers generates a large portion of total revenue
- High-value customers tend to make fewer but larger purchases

## Sales Trends
- Revenue varies over time, indicating potential seasonal demand
- Certain months show higher sales peaks

## Product Performance
- A limited number of products contribute significantly to revenue
- Sales distribution is uneven across product categories

## Business Implications
- Opportunity to improve customer retention strategies
- Potential to optimize marketing around high-performing products
- Need for deeper analysis of repeat purchase behavior
